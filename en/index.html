What We Do

Our Work Areas

We work across key pillars aimed at building a more informed and prosperous community.

Education & Civic Awareness

Empowering individuals through knowledge, civic training, and advocacy programs.

Indigenous Rights & Culture

Preserving Amazigh identity, language, and the rights of indigenous communities.

Peacebuilding

Promoting a culture of peace, non-violence, and effective conflict resolution.

Human Rights

Defending democratic values, freedom of speech, and universal human rights.

Innovation & Tech

Supporting youth entrepreneurship and training in modern technological tools.

Climate Responsibility

Advocating for environmental protection and sustainable climate practices.
