Our Identity

Knowledge Through Empowerment

EGAS (ⵉⴳⴰⵙ), based in Zuwara, Libya, is a youth-driven NGO founded to empower indigenous communities, youth, and women through education, awareness, and advocacy. The word EGAS means “knowledge” in Tamazight, reflecting the organization’s indigenous roots. It also functions as a discreet acronym for Education, Governance, Awareness, Sustainability.

EGAS works at the intersection of human rights, peacebuilding, and sustainable development, equipping marginalized groups with the tools to participate in decision-making, address societal challenges, and shape an inclusive future.
