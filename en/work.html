What We Do

Work Areas

We work across key pillars aimed at building a more informed and prosperous community

Education & Civic Awareness

Empowering individuals through knowledge and civic training, enabling active participation in society and understanding of rights.

Indigenous Rights & Culture

Preserving the Amazigh cultural identity, language, and heritage, and advocating for the rights of indigenous communities.

Peacebuilding

Promoting a culture of peace and non-violence through effective conflict resolution and peace education programs.

Human Rights

Defending democratic values, freedom of speech, and universal human rights through advocacy and accountability initiatives.

Innovation & Tech

Supporting youth and women in entrepreneurship and providing training on modern technological tools and digital innovation.

Climate Responsibility

Advocating for local environmental protection and raising awareness of climate change and sustainable ecological practices.
