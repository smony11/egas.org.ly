Strategic Direction

Mission & Vision

We work with firm values and a clear vision towards a more just and developed community

Our Vision

A peaceful and inclusive Libya where all communities, regardless of identity or background, can thrive with equal rights, opportunities, and representation.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized groups in Libya — especially indigenous peoples, youth, and women — through education, awareness, and training, enabling them to become active agents of peace, equality, and sustainable development.

Our Foundation

Core Values

The values that shape our identity and guide our daily work.

Education & Awareness

Innovation & Tech

Embracing tech solutions and youth entrepreneurship.

Climate Responsibility

What We Aim For

Strategic Goals

Ambitious goals we work to achieve step by step.

Political Participation

Raise awareness of democratic rights and encourage participation in local political life.

Youth Empowerment

Equip youth with skills and knowledge to lead their community's future.

Women Empowerment

Strengthen women's role in society economically, politically and culturally.

Amazigh Heritage

Preserve the Amazigh language and heritage and spread it among new generations.

Share Our Vision
